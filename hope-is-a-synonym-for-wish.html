Sinopsis

It all started with a missing AirPod. In November 2023, I traveled to Abu Dhabi to watch the F1 race. As a music lover, it was difficult to accept that I had probably left my AirPods at home. So, the first thing I did was buy a new pair. While I was there, a lady who had apparently watched one of my documentaries approached me and suggested I create a new one. She mentioned that the UAE is all about sand and malls, implying there was nothing interesting to film. This didn’t sit well with me. Challenge accepted. I began exploring the city and the country to discover more. It was far from being "just sand and malls." To me, it is the "Seven Deserts of Desires," and Abu Dhabi is synonymous with dreams.
